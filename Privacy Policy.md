# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: December 29, 2025**

## Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how The Simple Store ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our mobile game application (the "App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and complying with applicable privacy laws, including the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

Our App is designed for users of all ages, including children under 13. We take special care to protect the privacy of younger users.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide

We may collect the following information that you provide directly:
- **Account Information**: Username, age range (optional)
- **User Content**: Game progress, scores, achievements, and settings
- **Communications**: Messages you send to us for support or feedback

### Automatically Collected Information

When you use our App, we may automatically collect:
- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers
- **Usage Data**: Game statistics, session duration, features used, in-app interactions
- **Log Data**: IP address, crash reports, performance data
- **Advertising ID**: For non-personalized ads (can be reset in device settings)

### Information from Third Parties

We may receive information from:
- **Third-party services**: Analytics providers, advertising networks, payment processors
- **Social media platforms**: If you connect social accounts (with your permission)

## How We Use Your Information

We use collected information for:

1. **Providing and Improving the App**
   - Delivering game features and functionality
   - Personalizing user experience
   - Maintaining and improving App performance
   - Debugging and fixing technical issues

2. **Analytics and Research**
   - Understanding how users interact with the App
   - Analyzing trends and usage patterns
   - Conducting research to improve our games

3. **Communication**
   - Responding to support requests
   - Sending important updates about the App
   - Providing information about new features (with consent)

4. **Advertising**
   - Displaying age-appropriate advertisements
   - We only show non-personalized ads to users under 13

5. **Safety and Security**
   - Protecting against fraud and abuse
   - Enforcing our Terms of Service
   - Complying with legal obligations

## Children's Privacy

We take children's privacy seriously and comply with COPPA requirements:

### Users Under 13

- **Parental Consent**: We obtain verifiable parental consent before collecting personal information from children under 13
- **Limited Data Collection**: We collect only the minimum information necessary for the App to function
- **No Personalized Ads**: Children under 13 see only age-appropriate, non-personalized advertisements
- **No Social Features**: Chat or social features require parental consent
- **Parental Rights**: Parents can review, delete, or refuse further collection of their child's information

### How Parents Can Exercise Their Rights

Parents of children under 13 can:
- Review the personal information collected from their child
